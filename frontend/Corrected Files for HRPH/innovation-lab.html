How the Lab works
Four steps to better HR thinking.
1
Introduce yourself

Join the New Members area, share who you are and what you're working on — and find peers at your career stage.

2
Submit your ideas

Bring a real HR challenge, a new approach, or an experimental idea to the Idea Submission Board for peer review.

3
Discuss & debate

The Feedback & Discussion boards are where ideas get stress-tested — by people who understand the complexity of HR decisions.

4
Request mentorship

The Mentorship Request boards connect you with experienced HR practitioners for guidance on specific challenges and career questions.
